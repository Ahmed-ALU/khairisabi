About Our Company

We are an agri company who seeks to operate in the Barabarai rice productivity in Guinea while creating an eco-friendly environment for the community. With Khairi Sabi you can find your key of happiness.

Our core product is our locally-made parboiled rice machine, which is empowered by a Solar Panel that aims at supporting farmers in boiling their rice eco-friendly to produce Barabarai Rice and increase their production.

Objective

Design an eco-friendly Parboiled rice machine in order to reduce deforestation (tree cutting) due to the local transformation of paddy rice.
Scaling-up the barabarai rice production through automating the parboiling process.
Ascending the demand of local rice by making it affordable
Improving nutrition standard of the local population through availing organically produced, high nutritious local rice.

Aim of the Project

The primary aim of the project is to design an eco-friendly parboiled rice machine to reduce deforestation and improve the standard of living for local populations in Guinea. To achieve this, the project would focus on several key objectives, including automating the parboiling process to increase the production of local rice, making it more affordable and nutritious through organic production, and reducing poverty, hunger, and youth unemployment by 2045.

The impact of our company is multifaceted and far-reaching. By providing the local community with nutritious and high-quality rice, the company is contributing to improving the overall health and wellbeing of the population. In addition, the company's presence in the area is also creating employment opportunities for young people, which can have a positive effect on the local economy. Furthermore, the company's focus on local production also has a significant economic impact by reducing the need for importing rice and supporting the local economy. At the same time, the company's green farming practices, which are facilitated by the parboiled machine, promote sustainable agriculture, protecting the environment and preserving natural resources. Additionally, the parboiled machine supports the protection of mangroves and forests, which is critical for maintaining the ecological balance in the region. Overall, the impact of the agricultural company is significant in various aspects, including social, economic, and environmental.
